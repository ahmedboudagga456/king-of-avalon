About Mechtanium Surge

Mechtanium Surge is the final arc of the Bakugan Battle Brawlers series. It introduces new allies, powerful villains, the concept of Chaos Bakugan, and the use of high-tech battle suits.

  • Season: 4
  • Main Hero: Dan Kuso
  • Main Villain: Mag Mel
  • Bakugan Evolution: Titanium Dragonoid
